## STAGE DEFINITIONS

### 1. Lead (New)
- Submitted form but not contacted yet
- Action: Call within 24 hours

### 2. Contacted
- Spoke with lead
- Collecting info / qualifying
- Action: Determine if qualified

### 3. Qualified
- Meets criteria: 2+ yrs, $25K+/mo revenue, 620+ credit
- Has clear funding need
- Action: Collect docs, submit to lenders

### 4. Submitted
- Application sent to lender(s)
- Waiting for response
- Action: Follow up, keep client informed

### 5. Approved
- Lender approved, offer extended
- Action: Present offer to client, get signature

### 6. Funded
- Money deposited in client account
- Action: Confirm receipt, collect commission

### 7. Closed - Lost
- Didn't qualify, declined, or went elsewhere
- Action: Note reason, add to nurture list if appropriate

## CONVERSION TARGETS

| Stage | Target Conversion |
|-------|-------------------|
| Visitor → Lead | 5-10% |
| Lead → Contacted | 90%+ (just pick up the phone) |
| Contacted → Qualified | 40-60% |
| Qualified → Submitted | 80%+ |
| Submitted → Approved | 60-80% |
| Approved → Funded | 90%+ |

**Overall: 100 leads → 15-25 funded deals**
